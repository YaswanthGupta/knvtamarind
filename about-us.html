About Us

"K.N.VISWANATHAIAH & SON" is established in the year 1962 at Hindupur (Andhra Pradesh, India). We “K.N.VISWANATHAIAH & SON”, are highly engaged in exporting and importing of tamarind all over India and have achieved a remarkable position in the market.

We are currently exporting to Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra and ready to export all over India.

We import and export the following types of Tamarind:
  • Karpuli
  • Flower
  • Boat

Product Description:
Sweet and tangy, tamarind is one of the widely used spice-condiments found in every South-Asian kitchen!

Health benefits of Tamarind:
  • Tamarind fruit contain certain health benefiting essential volatile chemical compounds, minerals, vitamins and dietary fiber.
  • Tamarind is rich in tartaric acid.
  • This prized condiment spice is a good source of minerals like copper, potassium, calcium, iron, selenium, zinc and magnesium.

Medicinal uses of Tamarind:
  • It's pulp has been used in many traditional medicines as a laxative, digestive.
  • This spice condiment is also used as emulsifying agent in syrups, decoctions, etc., in different pharmaceutical products.
